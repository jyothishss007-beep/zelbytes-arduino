## Mounting Notes

- HC-SR04 ultrasonic sensor was hand-held during testing.
- Sensor was oriented horizontally and aimed directly at the target.
- Distance measurements were verified using a ruler.
- Target surface was a flat ruler/object placed in front of the sensor.
- Best readings were obtained when the target was perpendicular (approximately 90°) to the sensor face.
- Sensor and target were kept free of major obstructions.
- Reliable measurements were observed within the tested range of approximately 10 cm to 30 cm.
- Objects very close to the sensor (< 2 cm) may fall within the blind zone and produce unreliable readings.
- Angled or irregular surfaces can cause reflected sound waves and measurement errors.
