1. Arduino Uno

A microcontroller development board used to control electronic components.

Main specifications:

Microcontroller: ATmega328P
Operating Voltage: 5 V
Input Voltage (recommended): 7–12 V
Digital I/O Pins: 14 (6 PWM outputs)
Analog Input Pins: 6
Flash Memory: 32 KB
SRAM: 2 KB

2. LED (Light Emitting Diode)

An electronic component that emits light when current flows through it.

Specifications:

Type: Standard 5 mm LED
Forward Voltage:
Red LED → 1.8–2.2 V
Green/Blue → 2–3.3 V
Typical Current: 10–20 mA

3 . Push Button (Tactile Switch)

A momentary switch that closes the circuit only while pressed.

Specifications:

Type: Normally Open (NO)
Operating Voltage: 3–12 V (commonly used at 5 V)
Number of Pins: 4 (internally connected in pairs)
Mechanical operation: Momentary contact

4. 220 Ω Resistor

Limits current flowing through the LED to prevent damage.

Specifications:

Resistance: 220 ohms (Ω)
Power Rating: ¼ W (0.25 W) typical
Tolerance: ±5% (Gold band)
